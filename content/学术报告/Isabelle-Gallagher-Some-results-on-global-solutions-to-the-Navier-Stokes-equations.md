### 介绍

Navier-Stokes方程：

$$
\left\{\begin{aligned}
\partial_t u + u \cdot \nabla u - \Delta u & = - \nabla p, \quad \text{in}\; \mathbb{R}^3\\
\text{div} \  u &= 0
\end{aligned}\right.
$$
